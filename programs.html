Programs

Core Service Areas

Our programs focus on immediate relief and sustainable recovery.

Emergency Relief Program

Urgent essentials, transport support, and short-term stabilization.

  • Response in 24–48 hours
  • Priority for high-risk households
  • Rapid partner referrals

Community Care Initiative

Outreach visits and essential supply distribution to reduce isolation.

  • Monthly service days
  • Supply kits distribution
  • Follow-up support

Long-Term Recovery Support

Case support for education, employment, and family stability.

  • Skills referrals
  • Career support
  • Stabilization planning
