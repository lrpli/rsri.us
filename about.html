About RSRI

Mission & Vision

We aim to be a trusted, fast-response nonprofit that supports families beyond the crisis point.

Through coordinated resources, emergency relief, emotional support, and local partnerships, RSRI helps reduce the long-term impact of hardship and rebuild stable lives.
